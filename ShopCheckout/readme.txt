Use Case
Customer picks up a Basket.
Customer adds multiple items in the Basket.
ShopManager performs checkout by taking a Basket of items and outputs its total cost.


Actors
Basket
Item
ShopManager


Goal - to calculate total amount for the items in the basket


Pre-condition
Items include Bananas, Oranges, Apples, Lemons, Peaches.
Item's price is known before item is added to the Basket.


Trigger
Customer goes to checkout


Steps
1. Create Basket
2. Add items to the Basket
3. ShopManager to calculate total price of the Basket.
